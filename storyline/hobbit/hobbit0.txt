, are a Harfoot hobbit, living just outside of Hobbiton, in a Smial near the road to Bywater. You enjoy your simple life, which consists mostly of farming and eating (no less than six meals a day) and  putting off the unpleasant necessity of having to tidy up the big pile of lumber your great-uncle Fredegar has left in your backyard. One day, as you are moving what appears to be a giant jar of dried knawels, you notice a white ring underneath it, emanating its own mysterious light. What do you do? Do you pick up the strange ring or do you leave it there, preferring not  to associate yourself with such strange objects…
