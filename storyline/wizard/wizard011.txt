Since you don’t know what King’s Landing looks like and this terrible place has no floo networks or portkeys or even flying brooms, you are forced to travel to King’s Landing on foot. This takes you several months.
Immediately after you arrive at the capital, you are greeted by a blue-eyed innocent-looking little boy who tells you his master has waited for you and asks you to follow him. You have a choice before you – do you follow the little boy or do you refuse and go into the city on your own?
